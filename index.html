The Timberwolves are landing Rudy Gobert. The Jazz are landing four Minnesota first-round picks and 2022 first-round pick Walker Kessler in the deal for Gobert. (Source: Adrian Wojnarowski).

Dame D.O.L.L.A. For A Reason

Damian Lillard has signed a two-year $122 million extension with the Portland Trail Blazers, sources tell ESPN. The extension has a player option for the 2026-27 season.

Zion Secures The Bag

Zion Williamson, New Orleans Pelicans agree to five-year designated maximum rookie extension that could be worth up to $231 million. (Source: Austin Brown)

DEEEEEE-TROIT BASKET-BALL!

Jaden Ivey shows out in his NBA Summer League debut with the Detroit Pistons, delivering 20 points along with 6 rebounds and 6 assists in a win against the Portland Trailblazers. Fellow Pistons rookie Jalen Duren was also impressive despite limited play time, logging 9 points and 2 blocks.

Giddey Up

Upcoming Second-Year Thunder guard Josh Giddey dazzles in Summer League, posting a triple-double alongside the franchise's newest promising talent, Chet Holmgren. Giddey's 14 points, 10 rebounds, and 10 assists were instrumental in defeating the Memphis Grizzlies 87-71.
